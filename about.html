About ZerZeri

Our Story

Zer Zeri Educational Solutions, named from the Amharic phrase meaning "planting seeds," embodies a transformative vision to sow the seeds of holistic growth in students and educational systems. Founded with an unwavering commitment dedicated to reshaping how learning is perceived and delivered.

We believe education transcends traditional academic boundaries, by nurturing holistic development of students. We strongly advocate that true education plants the seeds for self-awareness, emotional intelligence, social intelligence, intellectual curiosity, creative innovation, physical well-being, and purpose, enabling students to flourish as innovative leaders and compassionate individuals.

Through our innovative approach program Roots, 21st Century Life Skills Course, play-based summer and afterschool programs, and meaningful parent-child interactions to foster deep connections and unlock students' inner potential.

Our consultancy services further support educational institutions by providing strategic guidance, innovative curricula, and technology integration to create inclusive, forward-thinking learning environments. By planting seeds of creativity, critical thinking, and emotional intelligence, Zer Zeri is dedicated to cultivating a generation of self-sufficient, purpose driven, responsible, and socially intelligent leaders who are equipped to shape a brighter future.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Innovation

Inclusivity

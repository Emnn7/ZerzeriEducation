About Zer Zeri Education

Planting seeds of fun and knowledge in young minds

Our Story

Zer Zeri Educational Solutions, named from the Amharic phrase meaning "planting seeds," embodies our vision to sow the seeds of curiosity and joy in learning.

Founded with a commitment to making education exciting, we believe learning should be an adventure that nurtures creativity and imagination in children.

Our Vision & Mission

Guiding principles that drive our educational solutions

Our Vision

A world where every child discovers the joy of learning through creative, engaging experiences.

Our Mission

To create fun, innovative educational programs that spark curiosity and inspire lifelong learning in children.
